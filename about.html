About Us

We are Group Animate from HC12, working on our group project for the course IM22503 Multimedia Web Programming (1–2025/2026). Our project, titled “Interactive Educational Website”, focuses on teaching 3D animation concepts through a visually engaging and interactive web platform.

This website allows users to explore 3D animation tutorials, learn from sample animation projects, and participate in interactive quizzes to test their understanding. By integrating multimedia elements such as videos, animations, and interactive components, we aim to provide an educational experience that is informative, engaging, and accessible across different devices.

All are welcome here, come on in!
